Bangladesh 2.0

Idea,innovation,vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

The vision of future
"Bangladesh 2.0" envisions a digitally advanced, economically robust, and socially inclusive nation by 2041. It focuses on digital transformation, sustainable development, and diversified economic growth, including IT and renewable energy sectors. Key goals include eradicating poverty, empowering marginalized communities, and modernizing infrastructure. The vision also emphasizes global engagement, climate resilience, and equitable access to healthcare and education.
Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Instruments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each human being is born with limitless potential, but our systems trap many in poverty. Poverty is not created by the poor; it’s a failure of institutions. If we can design inclusive systems that empower individuals, we can transform lives. Let’s build a world where no one is left behind, and poverty exists only in museums. Social business is the answer to many of the world’s problems—it’s about purpose, not profit. By unleashing the creativity and energy of people, we can solve global challenges. The future is in our hands; let’s make it a bright one for everyone."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus
Chief Adviser of the people's Republic of Bangladesh
